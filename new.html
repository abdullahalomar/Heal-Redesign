About Heal

Distraction-free space

A large 1-person space with an extra wide ergonomic seat and backrest to ensure safety, security and relaxation minimizing any distractions from the outside.

Vibrational therapy

Bold yet gentle tactile vibrations to enhance the auditory experience and help keep you in meditation.

Spatial audio

10-channel surround sounds tuned to healing frequencies to fully immerse you into to a meditative flow state.

Progress tracking
